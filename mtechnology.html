Ministry of Technology

What We Do

Responsibilities

The core areas this ministry oversees

Official Website
Develops and maintains nomashae.onrender.com — the Republic's official online presence, keeping it accurate, accessible, and up to date.
Minecraft Server
Manages the digital territory of Nomashae — the Minecraft world where cities, regions, and land ownership exist. Handles uptime, access, and expansion.
Digital Security
Ensures all of Nomashae's digital assets are secure and protected. Responsible for access control across official platforms and systems.
Software & Tools
Develops internal tools, scripts, and software that help run the Republic. Future home of the Jips programming language.
Data & Records
Maintains digital records of citizens, land ownership, decrees, and national assets. Ensures data is stored reliably and can be accessed when needed.
Communications
Oversees official communication channels — ensuring citizens and the government can communicate reliably across platforms.
