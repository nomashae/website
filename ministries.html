Active Ministries

Currently operational and serving the Republic

Ministry of Technology
Oversees the digital infrastructure of Nomashae, including the official website, Minecraft server, and any future digital systems. Responsible for keeping the nation's technology running, secure, and up to date.
Active
Ministry of Defence
Responsible for the protection and security of Nomashae. True to the Air Nomad philosophy, Nomashae's defence is built on de-escalation and peace — only acting when absolutely necessary.
Active
Ministry of Internal Affairs
Manages the day-to-day administration of Nomashae's internal regions, handles citizen relations, and ensures that the laws and decrees of the Republic are upheld across all territories.
Active
Ministry of Justice
Will establish Nomashae's own legal framework, currently operating under the Macedonian justice system. Responsible for drafting laws, handling disputes, and ensuring fairness and balance for all citizens.
Active
Nomashaelian Intelligence and Investigeishon Bureau
Internal security, investigation, and intelligence in service of the Republic.
Active
Ministry of Infrastructure & Public Works
Oversees the planning and construction of roads, buildings, and public utilities across Nomashae's cities and regions. Manages city development, land expansion, and the upkeep of public spaces in the Minecraft world.
Active
Nomashae Orbital & Space Agency
Nomashae looks beyond its borders — past the sky itself. NOSA is the Republic's agency for orbital research, satellite operations, deep-space exploration, and the defence of Nomashae from threats beyond the atmosphere.
