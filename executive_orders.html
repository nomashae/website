Press Release 1 — Culture, President & Flags
Press Release 24 Jan 2026 · 09:15 XST

Addressing New Flag

In order to make the flag of DMN Nomashae more modern and flowier, the flag will be updated to a newer 4K resolution design. The flag will mostly stay the same but the text and coat of arms element will be removed. DMN Nomashae will also receive its own coat of arms to better represent its citizens.

Addressing New President

DMN Nomashae has reinstated Krste Dimov as President. This comes after he was dismissed from his position wrongfully.

Addressing the Anthem

The anthem will not change in lyrics but will receive its own instrumental version and a combined vocal + instrumental version.

Closed 24 Jan 2026 · 10:27 XST Eftimij Novakovski
