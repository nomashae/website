Official Language

Flyingo Bisdomic

The living language of Nomashae

Flyingo Bisdomic is a constructed language developed alongside the Republic of Nomashae. Inspired by the Air Nomads' free-flowing speech, it simplifies English phonetics into a lighter, faster form — stripping syllables, reshaping vowels, and replacing complex words with rooted elemental vocabulary.

Writing System Phonetic Latin Based on English alphabet, simplified spelling
Word Order SVO Subject · Verb · Object — same as English
Core Influence English + ATLA Air Nomad vocabulary roots many key words
Status Active & Evolving New words added regularly by the Prime Minister

National Holidays

Days of peace, balance, and remembrance

Whole Spring
Avatar Day Festival
A season-long celebration honouring all Avatars past and present. Citizens add flowers to Avatar tombs and statues, reflect on balance, and gather in peace.
26th January
Flying Koi Carnival
A joyful carnival celebrating the beautiful flying koi of Nomashae's waters. A day of colour, community, and wonder — without the old-school thieves.
1st September
Republic Day
A holiday deticated to the establishment and creation of the republic. All the way back in September 1st, 2025.
