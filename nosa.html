Agency Pillars

The four core directives that guide every NOSA operation

Exploration & Research
NOSA conducts scientific research into orbital mechanics, celestial phenomena, and deep-space environments. Knowledge gathered beyond our atmosphere informs all of Nomashae's technological progress.
Satellite Communications
Maintaining and expanding Nomashae's orbital communication network. NOSA satellites ensure uninterrupted contact between all regions of the Republic, no matter the conditions on the ground.
Space Defence
Monitoring near-orbital space for threats — from rogue objects to hostile interference. NOSA coordinates with the Ministry of Defence to ensure Nomashae's sovereignty extends to the edge of the atmosphere.
Observation & Intelligence
NOSA's orbital assets provide critical ground observation data — monitoring Nomashae's regions, borders, and natural environment from above. Data is shared with the relevant ministries and the NIaI.
