Our Mission

The Nomashalian Intelligence & Investigation Bureau — NIaI — exists to protect the sovereignty, security, and citizens of the Republic of Nomashae. We investigate threats to national stability, gather intelligence to inform government decisions, and uphold the rule of law across all regions.

We operate with transparency where possible and discretion where necessary, always in service of the Republic's core values: balance, justice, and harmony.

What We Do

Operational Pillars

The four core areas that define the Bureau's work

Internal Security
Monitoring and protecting Nomashae from threats within its borders and regions. The Bureau maintains awareness of risks to national stability at all times.
Investigation
Conducting thorough, fair investigations into criminal and civil matters. Every case is handled with integrity and in accordance with Nomashalian law.
Intelligence
Gathering and analysing information to keep the government and citizens informed. Intelligence operations are conducted within the legal framework of the Republic.
Rule of Law
All Bureau operations are conducted within Nomashae's legal framework. The NIaI works in close coordination with the Ministry of Justice.
