Here is one full complex sentece both in Flying Bisonic & Flyingo Bisdomick:

Original English: Modern day is the best day for sky surfing. Also, I had an apple and a little chocolate bar.

Flying Bisonick (Ancient): ‚ÄúDiurnal moderna esh torar diurnal vola-bova. Nex, mi havar pomora e tikta chokolar barra.‚Äù
Breakdown: Diurnal moderna = modern day
esh torar = is best (greatest/strongest)
vola-bova = sky surfing (lit. fly-sky)
Nex = also / then
mi havar = I had
pomora = apple (constructed from pom- = fruit + -ora = red/round)
tikta chokolar barra = little chocolate bar

Modern Flyingo Bisdomick: ‚ÄúDiurn mod es tor diurn for bo-vo. Nex, mi had pom e tik chok bar.‚Äù
Breakdown: Diurn mod = modern day
es tor = is best for
bo-vo = for sky surfing (fly-sky)
Nex = also / next
mi had= I had
pom = apple
tik chok bar = little chocolate bar
