What We Do

Responsibilities

The three pillars of internal governance

Citizen Registration & Records
Maintains the official register of all Nomashae citizens — tracking names, aliases, roles, and status. Every citizen of the Republic is recorded and recognised here.
Regional Administration
Oversees the six ATLA-inspired regions of Nomashae — ensuring each territory is managed, represented, and developed in line with the Republic's values.
Complaints & Citizen Services
The first point of contact for citizens with concerns, suggestions, or disputes. Every complaint is heard, recorded, and addressed through proper channels.
Territory

Regions of Nomashae

Six regions inspired by the world of Avatar: The Last Airbender

Capital · All Elements
Uni Citi
The United City — capital of Nomashae and seat of the government. Where all four elements converge and all citizens are represented equally.
Water · Northern
Northern Water Tribe
Nomashae's northern territory, inspired by the grand Northern Water Tribe. Known for its resilience, adaptability, and the strength of its waterways.
Water · Southern
Southern Water Tribe
The southern reaches of Nomashae, reflecting the close-knit spirit of the Southern Water Tribe. Community-driven and deeply connected to the land.
Air · Eastern
Eastern Air Temple
High and open, the Eastern Air Temple region embodies freedom and elevation. A place of reflection, peace, and the nomadic spirit of the Air Nomads.
Air · Western
Western Air Temple
The western counterpart to the Eastern Air Temple. Known for its unique inverted architecture and its culture of harmony and open skies.
Earth · Central
Earth Kingdom
The vast and enduring heartland of Nomashae. The Earth Kingdom region is home to the Republic's most stable and established settlements.
Fire · Southern
Fire Nation
Nomashae's Fire Nation region — fierce, industrious, and driven. Once a symbol of conflict in ATLA's world, here it represents passion, ambition, and renewal.
How We Help

Citizen Services

What citizens can contact this ministry for

Citizen Registration
New to Nomashae? Contact the Ministry of Internal Affairs to be officially registered as a citizen of the Republic.
Region Assignment
Citizens can request assignment to a specific region of Nomashae. The ministry will process and record your regional affiliation.
Complaints & Disputes
Have a concern about governance, another citizen, or a regional issue? Submit a formal complaint and the ministry will investigate and respond.
Record Updates
Changed your alias, role, or region? Contact the ministry to update your official citizen record in the Republic's registry.
General Enquiries
Questions about Nomashae's structure, regions, or governance? The Ministry of Internal Affairs is your first point of contact.
