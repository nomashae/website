What We Do

Responsibilities

How the Ministry promotes and manages tourism across the Republic

Destination Promotion
Showcasing Nomashae's seven regions to the outside world — their landscapes, culture, and unique elemental character. Every region has a story worth telling.
Visitor Experience
Ensuring every visitor to Nomashae — whether in the Minecraft world or the digital Republic — has a welcoming, memorable, and worthwhile experience.
Events & Festivals
Organising national events, cultural festivals, and seasonal celebrations that reflect Nomashae's elemental calendar. From Avatar Day to the Fire Festival, every season has its tradition.
Maps & Guides
Producing and maintaining official maps, visitor guides, and regional information for all of Nomashae's territories. Every citizen and visitor deserves to know where they are.
Cultural Exchange
Facilitating cultural exchange between Nomashae and external communities. The Ministry welcomes visitors who come in the spirit of curiosity, learning, and shared experience.
Visitor Recognition
Acknowledging and celebrating visitors who contribute to Nomashae's community. Exceptional visitors may be recommended for honorary recognition by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.

Official Destinations

The seven regions of the Republic — each with its own element, landscape, and character

Air · Northern
Northern Air Temple
Perched high above the clouds, the Northern Air Temple is a place of meditation, freedom, and breathtaking vistas. Home to Nomashae's most philosophical citizens and a centre for Air Nomad tradition.
Scenic Meditation Architecture
Air · Eastern
Eastern Air Temple
A peaceful sanctuary known for its gardens and open courtyards. The Eastern Air Temple is currently undergoing restoration by the Ministry of Infrastructure — visitors are welcome to witness its renewal.
Gardens Restoration Peaceful
Air · Southern
Southern Air Temple
The birthplace of Nomashae's founding spirit. The Southern Air Temple is the most historically significant site in the Republic — a must-visit for anyone seeking to understand where Nomashae began.
Historical Founding Site Cultural
Air · Western
Western Air Temple
Unique among the Air Temples for its inverted architecture — built beneath the clifftops rather than above them. The Western Air Temple offers some of Nomashae's most dramatic and unusual views.
Unique Architecture Dramatic Views
Water · Northern
Northern Water Tribe
A city of ice and canals, the Northern Water Tribe is one of Nomashae's most architecturally stunning regions. Its walls, waterways, and moon-lit bridges draw visitors from across the Republic.
Ice Architecture Waterways Night Views
Water · Southern
Southern Water Tribe
A close-knit community built around resilience and warmth. The Southern Water Tribe is known for its welcoming spirit, communal spaces, and the best food in the Republic.
Community Food Welcoming
Earth · Central
Earth Kingdom
The heartland of Nomashae — vast, varied, and full of life. From great walled cities to rolling farmland and dense forests, the Earth Kingdom is the most geographically diverse region in the Republic.
Diverse Landscapes Cities Nature
Fire · Southern
Fire Nation
A land of volcanic landscapes, bold architecture, and passionate culture. The Fire Nation's energy and ambition are reflected in everything from its art to its cuisine — intense, memorable, and uniquely its own.
Volcanic Culture Cuisine
All Elements · Capital
Uni Citi — The Capital
The heart of Nomashae where all four elements converge. Home to the Parliament, the Government Quarter, and the Republic's main cultural institutions. Every visitor to Nomashae should begin and end in Uni Citi.
Capital Government Must Visit

Visitor's Guide

How to experience Nomashae — step by step

1
Apply for Citizenship or Visit as a Guest
You can explore Nomashae as a guest or apply for full citizenship through the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Citizens get access to more of the Republic — including governance participation and regional residency.
2
Choose Your Region
Nomashae has eight distinct regions, each with its own element and character. Pick the one that calls to you — whether you're drawn to the freedom of the Air Temples or the warmth of the Water Tribes.
3
Join the Community
Connect with Nomashae on Discord, explore the Minecraft world, and engage with The Daily Scroll to stay up to date with national events, festivals, and announcements.
4
Attend an Event
Nomashae runs seasonal festivals and national events throughout the year. The Ministry of Tourism publishes the national calendar — check it and plan your visit around one of our celebrations.
5
Explore & Contribute
The best visitors leave something behind — an idea, a creation, a connection. Nomashae welcomes all who come with curiosity and a desire to be part of something bigger than themselves.
How We Help

Citizen Services

What citizens and visitors can contact this ministry for

Request a Regional Guide
Citizens and visitors can request detailed guides for any of Nomashae's eight regions — history, landmarks, local customs, and what to see and do.
National Events Calendar
Get the full national events calendar — festivals, ceremonies, sporting events, and cultural celebrations — so you never miss a moment in Nomashae.
Propose a Tourism Initiative
Citizens can submit ideas for new tourism attractions, trails, events, or visitor experiences. The Ministry reviews all proposals and acts on the best ones.
Cultural Exchange Requests
External communities or individuals seeking a cultural exchange with Nomashae can reach out to the Ministry. We welcome visitors who come in the spirit of learning and connection.
